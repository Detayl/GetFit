Get Fit With League of Legends
Get Fit with League of Legends is a workout calculator that takes your stats from your previously played game and calculates how many pushups/situps/squats you would have to do. It works for any of the regions you can play on and has an intensity modifier that allows you to determine just how hard you want to workout. This pulls the information directly from their servers so all of the hard work is done for you, simply enter your name, the workout you choose, and the intensity you'd like to try and click generate. Now working out has become a lot more fun!
