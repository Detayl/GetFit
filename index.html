Get Fit with League of Legends! The World's First Workout Program Generator based on your game results!

GFWL was designed and thought up by Detayl (Summoner, NA).

GFWL

The program generates a workout based on your in-game performance. This is generated based on your stats in comparison to your teammates. This is calculated by simply taking your score from the team highs/lows and the difference is multiplied by either 5 or 10, depending on the workout.

This program is in the beta stages and still has some work to be done to it in order for it to be a polished product but will be maintained by Detayl.
